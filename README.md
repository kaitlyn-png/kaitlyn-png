# 💫 About Me:
Hi! My name is Kaitlyn Eng and I am passionate about connecting programming and design. Currently I am pursing a B.S in Computer Science from UC Santa Cruz. With background experience with multiple programming languages (HTML, CSS, Python and C++), I aim to develop websites and create programs for clients. Outside of academics, I am actively involved in managing Slug Gaming's Valorant Team. As a participating officer, I strive to organize community events and tournaments as well as connect with club members to create a friendly environment for gamers. These experiences have assisted me by developing my management skills, improving my communication, and leading group projects. I am excited to work on projects and develop game centric programs! Let's connect!
